Reconnection

2025-2026 Workshop Project (WIP)

Role: Tech Lead/UI Designer

Reconnection is a game about old friends reconnecting through a tabletop RPG akin to Dungeons and Dragons. It is a narrative-driven game that emphasizes the choices the player makes when interacting with their fellow players. This project consists of 8 team members, and is being developed in Unreal Engine.

My role in this project is the Tech Lead, where I oversee what is being implemented in engine, who is currently in engine, and managing when we build our project. I am also a UI designer and level designer for this project. For UI design, I have worked on designing layouts for our combat screen, Real World Scenes, Dialogue, and Exploration Scenes. I am also responsible for adding functionality to these menus through Unreal's Blueprints. Working with our only programmer, we came up with a dialouge system for our game utilizing blueprints and the comabt system reminiscent of old school RPGS. I have also designed the layout for our settings menu, as well as implementing functionality to the menu through blueprints.
